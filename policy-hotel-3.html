Smoking

A fee of up to $250 will be assessed for smoking in a non-smoking room. Please ask the Front Desk for locations of designated outdoor smoking areas.

Refunds

There will be a cancellation fee charged to the user if they decide to cancel their reservation. A pop-up will warn users before the fee is in effect.

Payment Fee

By using our facilities, there may be an additional fee that users must pay on the last day of their visit. Other additional fees include room service, or consumes the snacks/drinks that are provided in the mini bar of each hotel room.
